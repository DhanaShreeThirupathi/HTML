Chocolate chip cookies

Prep time:15 minutes

Cook time:10 minutes

Steps:
  1. Prepare the oven to 350oF(180oC).
  2. Mix the flour and sugar in the bowl.
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
Ingredients:
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate chips 1 cup

Enjoy your homemade cookies!
